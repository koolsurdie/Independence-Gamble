Welcome to The Independence Gamble

🏭 Aureon Devices — A 140-Year Legacy

For nearly 14 decades, Aureon Devices has been synonymous with precision electronics. Founded in 1880, the company built its reputation manufacturing components for aerospace, defense, and industrial equipment—markets where reliability was not a luxury but a legal requirement.

Until last year, Aureon was a division of a much larger conglomerate. But the parent company made a decision: divest. Aureon was spun off, handed the keys to independence, and told to survive on its own.

The Problem: The markets Aureon dominated—aerospace, defense, industrial—are in structural decline. Cheaper alternatives exist. New entrants are flooding the space. The supply chain is fragile. And disruption is coming.

Your Challenge: You have 10 years (20 strategic decisions) to navigate five forces of competitive pressure while managing four critical KPIs: cash strength, market share, supply stability, and disruption readiness. Every choice has trade-offs. Every decision shapes the future.

🎯 The question is simple: Can Aureon Devices remain independent?
